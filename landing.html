Features

Everything you need to stay focused

Offdem comes with powerful features designed to help you eliminate distractions and boost your productivity.

Customizable Block List

Add any distracting website to your block list with just a few clicks. Easily manage which sites to block including social media, news, entertainment, or any other distracting websites.

Flexible Timer Control

Set custom time periods for blocking distracting websites. Choose how long you want to stay focused with timers for 25 minutes, 1 hour, or customize your own time period.

Strict Blocking Mode

Once activated, sites remain blocked until your timer expires. No cheating allowed! When strict mode is enabled, you won't be able to access blocked sites until your focus time is complete.

Motivational Messages

Receive encouraging reminders when you try to access blocked sites. Instead of distractions, you'll see motivational quotes and reminders of your goals.
